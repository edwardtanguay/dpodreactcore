Good Arguments

A guide to understanding the intellectual and ethical rot in Americans' minds, lives, culture, religions and government

by Edward Tanguay

This book is a sequel to a book I began writing in 2016 called Raising the Intellectual and Ethical Bar in the American Culture which I abandoned in 2020 after years of research engaging in argumentative conversations with Americans online and off revealed that the goal of the book is impossible without a structural change in the American character (for insights into the American character, see Kurt Andersen's Fantasyland: How America Went Haywire: A 500-Year History).

The book Good Arguments is the result of what any curious person would do who grew up in a culture that is corrupt and dysfunctional to the point of hopelessness: investigate and expose the reasons why the culture is corrupt and dysfunctional with the goal of forcing a change in people who profit from the culture being corrupt and dysfunctional.
